B2B SaaS Case Study

Reducing Customer Acquisition Cost For A B2B SaaS Company

How connecting paid media to CRM and revenue data cut CAC 35 percent while improving lead quality across the funnel.

0%
Reduction in Customer Acquisition Cost
The Challenge

Healthy Lead Volume, Unsustainable Acquisition Costs

A rapidly growing B2B SaaS company was investing heavily in paid media to drive new customer acquisition, but rising advertising costs and inconsistent lead quality were limiting growth. While lead volume was healthy, the cost to acquire new customers had become unsustainable, making it difficult to scale profitably. The objective was to lower customer acquisition cost, improve lead quality, and create a more efficient growth engine without reducing overall lead volume.

Our Approach

A Full-Funnel Strategy Built Around Paying Customers, Not Just Leads

We developed a full-funnel acquisition strategy designed to optimize every stage of the customer journey, from the initial click to becoming a paying customer. Our strategy included:

  • Rebuilding Google Search campaigns around high-intent commercial keywords
  • Launching LinkedIn and Meta campaigns targeting decision-makers by industry, company size, and job function
  • Integrating CRM data to optimize campaigns based on qualified opportunities and closed customers instead of top-of-funnel leads
  • Refining audience segmentation using first-party data and lookalike modeling
  • Implementing AI-driven bidding strategies focused on downstream revenue metrics
  • Continuous testing of ad messaging, landing pages, offers, and conversion paths
  • Budget optimization across channels based on pipeline contribution rather than click volume

Every optimization was measured against one objective: acquiring more customers at a lower cost while maintaining lead quality.

The Results

Measurable Improvements Across The Business.

0%
Reduction in Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Higher-quality leads entering the sales pipeline
  • Improved lead-to-opportunity conversion rates
  • Lower cost per qualified demo across paid channels
  • Increased marketing efficiency without reducing growth
  • Greater visibility into marketing's impact on revenue

By aligning campaign optimization with CRM and sales performance data, marketing dollars were invested where they generated the greatest business impact.

Why It Worked

Most B2B Campaigns Optimize For Leads. We Optimized For Customers.

By connecting advertising platforms with CRM and revenue data, we were able to identify which campaigns, keywords, audiences, and creatives consistently generated paying customers rather than simply filling the top of the funnel. This allowed us to:

  • Prioritize high-value customer segments
  • Eliminate wasted spend on low-converting traffic
  • Improve sales and marketing alignment
  • Scale campaigns based on revenue performance
  • Build a repeatable customer acquisition strategy for long-term growth
The Bottom Line

Sustainable Growth Depends On Acquiring Customers Efficiently, Not Simply Generating More Leads.

Through advanced paid media strategy, AI-powered optimization, and revenue-focused measurement, we helped this SaaS company reduce Customer Acquisition Cost by 35 percent, improve lead quality, and build a scalable acquisition program that accelerated profitable growth.
